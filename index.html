About Us

Pitamber Buddh Vihar Samiti is a registered society working for social welfare, education, health, and community development.

Address: Bargadi Nagar Panchayat, Bakshi Ka Talab, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh

Our Work

Education Development

Providing education, training, and support for children and youth including schools, vocational training, and higher education opportunities.

Health & Medical Services

Organizing health camps, awareness programs, and providing free medical support to underprivileged communities.

Women Empowerment

Skill training programs and self-employment opportunities for women to promote independence and dignity.

Community Development

Improving rural infrastructure, sanitation, and overall community welfare initiatives.

Social Awareness

Campaigns against social evils like dowry, addiction, and inequality while promoting harmony.

Environment Protection

Tree plantation drives and awareness programs for environmental conservation.

Disaster Relief

Providing help during natural disasters and emergencies to affected people.

Youth Development

Training and empowering youth through education, sports, and skill development programs.
