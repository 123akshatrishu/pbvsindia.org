About Us

Pitamber Buddh Vihar Samiti is a registered society working for social welfare, education, health, and community development in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh.

Our Work

Education Support

Providing free education and awareness for underprivileged students.

Health Services

Organizing medical camps and healthcare awareness programs.

Women Empowerment

Skill training and self-employment support for women.

Social Awareness

Campaigns against addiction, illiteracy, and inequality.

Environment Protection

Tree plantation and cleanliness drives.

Community Development

Helping poor families and supporting rural development.
